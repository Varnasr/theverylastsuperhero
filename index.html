The Very Last Superhero

An illustrated speculative fiction novel set in the ruins of Delhi, The Very Last Superhero follows 14-year-old Sumati through climate collapse, surveillance, and the rebirth of memory.

Characters & Lore

Sumati

The 14-year-old protagonist who carries the Salt Lamp and rekindles the Resistance archive.

Chandra

A mysterious crow who watches over the group and guides Sumati.

The Firebird

A symbol of reawakening that only returns when everything else has failed. It marks the rebirth of truth and memory.

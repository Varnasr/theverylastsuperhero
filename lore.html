Salt Lamps

Technology | Archive Memory System

Forged from crystalline Himalayan salt, these lamps hold more than light — they record, store, and relay the living oral histories of the Resistance. Used across safehouses, each lamp is part of a decentralized network. When Sumati activates a lamp, she accesses not only history, but warnings, blueprints, and lost testimonies.
